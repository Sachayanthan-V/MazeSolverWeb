PROBLEM STATEMENT

FUNCTIONS OF AGV :
         AGV [Automated Guided Vehicle] . It is device or a Vehicle is used for transporting a things or product or a raw material from one place to another place using path tracker . path trackers such as line follower, gps and etc.,
         Speed and motion of the vehicle are fully programmed by the programmer based on line follower or an gsm.
         And extra facilities of the agv is obstacle avoidance to safely transport the products inside the industries. The obstacle avoidance is used to find any kind of obstacles are in these path and identifies the distance and safely stops the vehicles before hitting into it. The obstacle in industries such as humans walking, and some other agv flowing in these area , and etc.,

PROBLEM OCCURING :
         The obstacle avoidance is only used for flowable or movable objects , because at this time the agv stops it functioning. Therefore the obstacle is fixed or non-movable object the agv doesn't moves from here upto the object are cleared by human manually. This type of critical situation at industries causes the big lag to the production in industries.

SOLUTION

         According to the problem occurs on the AGV for the static or non-movable objects or a goods, the agv does not want to wait until clearing the path. so we need upgrade the agv into next level. so we want to combine the agv with maze solver capability. In case the agv meets the critical situation , the agv detects it static or movable obstacle, and if it is movable the agv works normally , else it acts as a maze solver to find the next path to reach the destination with shortest distance and move according to it.
         so we create a device or a vechicle. The device is combination of [Advance Agv] agv and maze solver.
